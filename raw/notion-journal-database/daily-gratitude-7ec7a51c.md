Personal Wins:

- I communicated in a calm way with my fiance about his behavior today and it was received with pure positivity! I saw a story about how a Chinese woman succeeds in life is her way of communicating, she said doesn't matter how urgent, frustrated or upset you are, in every situation you can find a way to communicate calmly to make it effective. It is such a simple concept, But hard to practice. But Since I started to be more mindful about my emotions, I am able to catch myself before I get too impacted by my emotions and then move on to communicate in a fact Based, calm and respectful way. You intention is not to express your EMOTIONS, But rather give constructive feedbacks to how his/her action impact others and provide a way to let the other person feel they are respected. This is so hard but I feel like when I talked to my fiance about how he was behaving after Coming home from gym could make my dad feels like he wasn't being a good host. I told him I know he is hungry after Coming back from gym but he should not have been saying “since You haven't cooked I just bought food so we can eat quickly”. And then hurried onto eating ferociously. When I told him how I saw his actions could impact how my dad feels, he immediately said he understands And that was the end of the conversation. I still feel a bit protective of my dad obviously but I am quite satisfied with how the conversation turned out and hoping This will help pave the way for better communication in the future.

# Love is a skill

- Notion page id: `ec97a51c-1360-83f9-a7a7-01d759d0098c`

To be able to feel loved, and to be able to give love are both very different skills.

My parents, their actions if taking out of the context of the timeline and persistency, might be Things anyone can do and call that love

But the truth is, love is all these little things you do with persistancy.

And Why I feel so much love is also because I can feel loved in a profound way. When my dad said :”I'm so sorry that I brought so much trouble to you” there are so many mixed feelings bubbling up. But because I know how to feel loved, I know this is love. When someone shows you their vulnerability, and is even apologizing for causing trouble to their own daughter? If someone does not love you deeply, they will not be able to do this.

My heart is filled with love.

Sometimes I hear my fiance say that he feels “loved” or rather “being cared for” Based on material things. For example, he would say that my parents are “useless” during the period that we were living together during my post-surgery. This was very hurtful for me because when I think about it, the love I felt and the love my parents had shown for me is not as simple as something that he sees.

And I would think back on how he describes the importance of his friends and how much he needs to see them, spend a few hours to chat etc. This suddenly gave me an epiphany:

When he needs to relax and he goes to meet his friends, have some drinks, dinner, chat.

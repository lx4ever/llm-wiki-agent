# Mind and Body - All In

- Notion page id: `4347a51c-1360-82cf-9e61-01dc3c1f987f`

Today i Just had an epiphany while I was on the treadmill.

- Why do we try to distract our mind when we are doing aerobics?

- Heart is the strongest and hardest working muscles we have, yet we don't give it our full attention when we are exercising.

- It is so important to listen to your heart, feel your heart beat, blood pumping, and breath in and out while you are doing aerobics, especially when you are not distracted.

- Today when I started to listen to my body, I did 1min of plank like it was nothing. All I did was breathing in, feel my body straight, and my spine, Every muscle that supports this posture, and closed my eyes to feel my body doing its work, its miracle. It is such an incredible feeling to fully appreciate your body and how beautifully strong It is.

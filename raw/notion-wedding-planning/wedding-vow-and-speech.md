## Wedding Speech Final

Long long, as we stand here today, I can't help but reflect on our journey together. To be honest, I was never keen on getting married. After being a bridesmaid three times and attending countless weddings, I saw more disappointments than dreams come true. But when I am with you, one plus one felt better than being on my own.

I think you are very late. I have been waiting to be at this altar since our freshman year in university, when I first asked if you wanted to marry me. Because you were an idiot back then, you did not answer. I swore to myself that I would never ask another person to marry me unless he proposed first.

Ever since we reunited after ten years, I waited so long for you to propose that I almost considered proposing to myself just to speed things up! Then, finally, you did it, under the setting sun in Riverdale Park East, with about a million other people around.

The beautiful thing about life is that when you truly want something, the entire universe conspires to help you achieve it. It is never about what you are; it is about what you could be. I could be anything, but I wanted to be yours.

I will be your friend, your cheerleader, and your rock. We're not perfect, but the beauty of marriage is striving to be better for each other every day. We are not the perfect couple, but we are perfect for each other.

I haven't written anything worthwhile since my thesis, and I resisted the urge to ask ChatGPT to write this vow for me. You know, it is funny how life changes and how rare it is to set time aside for someone. But here I am, doing that just for you.

So here’s to us. To our new journey together. I will always, honestly, truly, and completely love you.

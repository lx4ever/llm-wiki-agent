Planning this wedding was like trying to assemble IKEA furniture without instructions. Absolutely nothing about this wedding was easy! But here we are, ready to take on one of the biggest undertakings of our lives.

I am so grateful for everyone who showed up today and celebrate with us. All of you are our most valued people in our lives.

我非常感谢今天出席并和我们一起庆祝的每个人。在座的每一位朋友和家人都是我们生命中最珍贵的人。

首先想感谢我最爱的爸爸妈妈，不光给予了我顽强不息的生命，也教会了我如何去爱，以及做一个值得被爱的人。

我要感谢我的新爸爸，马传起，第一次来到遥远的加拿大是为了我们的婚礼。而且最重要的是, 养育了马龙，让他成为了一个值得我托付终身的人。

我要感谢我的蓓蓓姐姐，我的大姑，还有我可爱的侄子66。你们第一次坐20多小时的飞机，不远万里从中国来参加我的婚礼。你们的到来对我来说比任何礼物都重要。

I want to thank my bridesmaids, Jingjing, Vera and my cousin, You guys have been the most thoughtful, understanding and supportive friends anyone could ever ask for.

I want to thank the groomsmen and everyone who helped to make this venue looking beautiful for our special day.

A very special thanks to our MC Eric, and our DJ/Drone Pilot/Videographer Anran tonight. Eric, thank you for being such a lively and wonderful MC tonight, please try this as a side hustle in the future! And Anran, thank you for you taking on so many roles last minute and done a tremendous job!

And, Last but not least, I want to thank to my husband.

We have compromised so much as a couple in these past few months. You pulled me out of my comfort zone by making us practice a wedding dance, when initially, I wanted us to sing “Love the Way You Lie,” with you singing Rihanna and me as Eminem. I am still terrible at dancing, but you made me a little bit better at it.

In the last few months, we fought and made up and then fought some more. There were times when I thought we'd never get through another fight. But you always came around, and here we are, stronger than ever.

Urban Dictionary defines love as, "The act of caring and giving to someone else. Having someone's best interest and wellbeing as a priority in your life." This definition resonates deeply with me. Love isn't just about the grand gestures, but the small, everyday acts of kindness and care. It is the smell of warm breakfast, the tender hug when I'm stressed, and the shared laughter at the end of a long day. Love is in the little moments that make up our everyday lives.

Marriage isn't a fairytale; it's an adventure that I'm excited to embark on with you.

I love the way you squint your eyes into a slit when you laugh and every streak of white in your hair. I love the way you cooperate with all my crazy ideas. I love your empathy, your kindness, and your unwavering support. Thank you for letting me be a better person and partner.

Here's to many more years of shared dreams, laughter, and countless adventures. I can't wait to see what the future holds for us.

祝愿我们在未来的岁月里，共同分享梦想、欢笑和无数的冒险，一起创造我们的未来。

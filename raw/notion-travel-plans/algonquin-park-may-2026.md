# 🏛️ Attractions & Trails Detail

## Centennial Ridges Trail ⭐ Top Pick

- Distance: 10.4 km loop

- Difficulty: Challenging

- Time: ~4–5 hours

- Location: Km 37.4 on Hwy 60

- Highlights: Two ridgelines with panoramic views over hundreds of sq km of Algonquin wilderness — widely considered the best hike in the park

- May note: Some sections may be muddy — wear waterproof hiking boots

## Mizzy Lake Trail ⭐ Best for Wildlife

- Distance: 11 km loop

- Difficulty: Moderate

- Time: ~4 hours

- Location: Km 15.4 on Hwy 60

- Highlights: 9 ponds and lakes, excellent moose, beaver, and loon sightings. Numbered interpretive posts.

- May note: Very muddy — waterproof boots essential. Pets not allowed.

## Spruce Bog Boardwalk — Easy Morning Walk

- Distance: 1.5 km loop

- Difficulty: Easy, barrier-free sections

- Location: Km 42.5 on Hwy 60

- Highlights: Black Spruce bog, carnivorous Pitcher Plants, excellent birding

## Lookout Trail — Best Short Hike

- Distance: 1.9 km loop

- Difficulty: Moderate (steep)

- Location: Km 39.7 on Hwy 60

- Highlights: Magnificent panoramic view over several hundred sq km of Algonquin. ~1 hour.

## Two Rivers Trail — Near Campground

- Distance: 5.8 km loop

- Location: Between km 30–31 on Hwy 60 (near campground)

- Difficulty: Moderate

## Algonquin Visitor Centre

- Location: Km 43 on Hwy 60

- Features: Museum exhibits, fire tower lookout, small café, bookstore, nature programs

- Hours: Check [ontarioparks.ca](http://ontarioparks.ca/) closer to date

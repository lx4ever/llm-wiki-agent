## The Arcade Cleveland

- Address: 401 Euclid Ave, Cleveland OH 44115

- Hours: Daily 7am–7pm · Free to explore

- What it is: America's first indoor shopping mall (1890) · National Historic Landmark · Inspired by Milan's Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II · 300-ft glass skylight atrium, 100 ft high · Hyatt Regency inside · Food court + 1890 Restaurant

- Fun fact: A scene from the 2025 Superman film was shot inside

- Budget: 30–45 min · free

## Playhouse Square

- Address: 1501 Euclid Ave, Cleveland OH 44115 · ☎ 216-241-6000

- What it is: World's 2nd largest theatre district (after Lincoln Center) · 12 historic theatres · Giant outdoor chandelier sign

- How to visit: Free to walk the district · Check [playhousesquare.org](http://playhousesquare.org/) for show listings

- Budget: 30 min stroll · right next to The Arcade on Euclid Ave

## Public Square

- Budget: 15–20 min · 5 min walk from Playhouse Square

# 30-Day Menu for Graves' Disease

This menu is designed for a 37-year-old Asian female with Graves’ disease, focusing on low-iodine, anti-inflammatory, gluten-free foods to support hyperthyroidism management. Consult a doctor before making dietary changes.

## Day 1

Breakfast: Rice porridge (congee) with ginger, scallions, and 1 poached egg. Side of fresh papaya.
Snack: 2 Brazil nuts.
Lunch: Grilled chicken breast with steamed broccoli and carrots. Jasmine rice with olive oil. Chamomile tea.
Snack: Sliced cucumber with turmeric and black pepper.
Dinner: Stir-fried turkey with bok choy, bell peppers, and shiitake mushrooms in a garlic-coconut aminos sauce. Brown rice.
Dessert: Fresh blueberries.

## Day 2

Breakfast: Smoothie with banana, spinach, 1 tbsp peanut butter, and unsweetened almond milk.
Snack: 1 apple.
Lunch: Quinoa salad with chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and lemon-olive oil dressing. Steamed kale.
Snack: Handful of pumpkin seeds.
Dinner: Baked freshwater salmon with ginger-turmeric glaze. Stir-fried green beans and carrots, sticky rice.
Dessert: Sliced mango.

## Day 3

Breakfast: Overnight chia pudding with coconut milk, topped with strawberries and crushed walnuts.
Snack: 1 orange.
Lunch: Turkey-vegetable soup with cabbage, carrots, and zucchini. Homemade broth (no iodized salt). Gluten-free rice noodles.
Snack: Sliced avocado with black pepper.
Dinner: Grilled pork tenderloin with roasted Brussels sprouts and 1/2 medium potato. Drizzle with olive oil.
Dessert: Fresh pineapple chunks.

## Day 4

Snack: Sliced pear with handful of almonds.

# Additional Notes

- Hydration: Drink water, herbal teas (e.g., chamomile, mint), or coconut water. Avoid caffeinated beverages.

- Portion Sizes: Adjust to prevent weight loss. Example: 1–1.5 cups rice/quinoa per meal, 4–6 oz protein, 1–2 cups vegetables.

- Cooking Tips: Use non-iodized salt or skip salt. Flavor with fresh herbs, ginger, turmeric, garlic, coconut aminos.

- Vitamin D: Ensure intake through fortified almond milk or 10–15 minutes sunlight exposure. Consult a doctor for supplementation.

- Soy Timing: Tofu (Days 5, 12, 17, 26) should be consumed 4 hours apart from thyroid medications.

- Potatoes: Included in moderation (Days 3, 10, 19, 29) to manage glycemic impact, paired with protein/fats.

- Meal Prep: Batch-cook rice, vegetables, and proteins to avoid processed foods with hidden iodine or gluten.

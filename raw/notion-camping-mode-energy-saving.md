Final Answer

To turn off non-essential functions in Camp Mode on a 2024 Tesla Model Y:

1. Activate Camp Mode and set to 18°C with fan speed at 1 or 2.

1. Disable seat heaters (all seats off).

1. Turn off steering yoke/wheel heating.

1. Set ambient lighting to Off or 0% brightness.

1. Dim the touchscreen to minimum brightness, mute audio, and avoid app usage.

1. Disable Sentry Mode and Cabin Overheat Protection.

1. Minimize phone key/Bluetooth activity.

With these settings, Camp Mode uses ~0.3–0.4 kW/hour, yielding ~86 km (53 miles) of range over 10 hours on a 16A, 120V charger in April Ontario weather (0–10°C). If outdoor temperatures drop (e.g., 0°C), range may reduce to ~79–82 km (49–51 miles).

Tips

- Monitor Power: Use the Energy app on the touchscreen to confirm Camp Mode’s draw stays low (~300–400 W).

- Campground Setup: Ensure the TT-30 outlet is stable. Use a surge protector and set charging to 16A via the Tesla app to avoid breaker trips.

- Further Savings: If 18°C feels comfortable, try 16–17°C to reduce heating load, potentially adding 5–10 km range.

- Interface Notes: The 2024 Model Y’s UI may vary slightly with software updates. If a setting is hard to find, use the Search function (magnifying glass) on the touchscreen.

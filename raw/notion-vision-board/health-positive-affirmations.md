# Health Positive Affirmations

- Notion page id: `9377a51c-1360-8283-b7ce-813b02bb9521`

1. I am [healing](https://parade.com/1031006/kelseypelzer/healing-bible-verses-scriptures/) my body with each deep breath.

2. My body is healthy and strong.

3. My body is powerful and knows how to heal itself.

4. My positive thoughts and actions renew my health and body.

1. My body is sacred and I will take care of it.

1. I exercise to honor my body’s strength.

1. My body is perfect the way it is.

1. I am healthy, optimistic, and happy.

1. My immune system is strong and keeps me safe.

1. I eat food to nourish and celebrate my body.

1. I sleep soundly and peacefully.

1. I crave new experiences and adventures.

1. I am worthy of good health.

1. I treat my body with the love and kindness it deserves.

1. I deserve to live a long, healthy life.

1. I release all doubts and insecurities.

1. I choose to think positively.

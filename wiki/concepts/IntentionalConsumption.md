Decision filter favoring needs/value over impulse wants.

In this wiki context, it means pausing purchases, checking alignment with medium-term goals, and evaluating utility-per-dollar before spending. It also emphasizes reducing emotional or social-triggered buying by using simple rules, waiting periods, and category budgets.

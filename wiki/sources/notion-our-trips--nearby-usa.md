## Summary
### 1. Niagara Falls, New York - Distance from GTA: ~1.5 hours (~130 km from Toronto via the Queenston-Lewiston Bridge) - Why visit: While the Canadian side of Niagara Falls is famous for its views, the U.S. side offers a more natural experience. You can explore Niagara Falls State Park, walk along the Niagara Gorge, and take the Maid of the Mist boat tour to get close to the falls.

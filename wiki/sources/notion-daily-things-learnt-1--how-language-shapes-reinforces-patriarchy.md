## Summary
Yesterday I had a row with my husband, I forceful told him to stop watching short videos and he told me off by saying “shut up, bitch”. I was so shocked and hurt at the time I couldn't react. I wasn't sure if it was the words, or the person who said it. These words are so vulgar, indecent and cruel that I could not believe it was said to another human being in my house. Then I thought about all the similar words to describe a man who is despicable, in English it might be “motherfucker”, “son of a bitch”, “dick”, “piece of shut”. But in Chinese, I found there are so little curse words for men (maybe because I am not very educated in this), all of the swear words I know are all describing women. And this infuriated me. Language is what shapes Our minds and actions in the beginning, and it is ingrained in the Chinese traditional culture that women gets attacked verbally more than men simp because there ARE no words to describe a despicable man. Oh How I wish more people would realize this, especially the men.

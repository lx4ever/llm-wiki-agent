## Summary
Word count: 213 As I write this letter, a testament for my descendants or any future reader, I ponder the limitless nature of familial love. My brother Thomas and I shared a profound bond, as deep as the one we had with Damien, our servant's son. Despite Damien's distorted physical appearance, his heart was pure, and we welcomed him as our own. Yet, societal norms and unspoken prejudices slowly wedged us apart.

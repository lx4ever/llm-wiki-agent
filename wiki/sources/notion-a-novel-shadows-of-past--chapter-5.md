## Summary
### Chapter 5: Elara's Isolation The golden leaves of autumn were giving way to the stark, bare branches of early winter when I next visited the manor. The air was crisp, and a thin layer of frost covered the ground, glistening in the morning sun. It was on this cold, clear day that I came to understand the depths of Elara's isolation. Elara was in the conservatory, a glass-walled sanctuary filled with exotic plants that thrived despite the chill outside. She sat at a grand piano, her fingers moving gracefully over the keys, coaxing out a melancholy melody that filled the room with a haunting beauty.

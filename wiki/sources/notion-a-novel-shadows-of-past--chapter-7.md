## Summary
### Chapter 7: The Unraveling With the truth of Thomas’s fate heavy on my heart, I sought out Lillian Grayson, the one person who might help make sense of the darkness enveloping the manor. The time travel brought me to her, a beacon in the fog of uncertainty. In her study, surrounded by books and the scent of burning sage, Lillian listened intently as I recounted my findings. Her face, usually so composed, reflected the gravity of my words.

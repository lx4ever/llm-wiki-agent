## Summary
### Chapter 9: The Horrifying Truth The final pieces of the puzzle fell into place on a night when the moon hung low in the sky, casting an eerie light over the manor. I had returned to the crypt, compelled by a need to uncover the last of Damien's secrets. There, hidden behind a false wall, I found what I dreaded yet expected – a small chamber, its walls adorned with clippings of the missing people and macabre trophies from his victims. In the center lay a diary, its pages filled with Damien's twisted thoughts and confessions.

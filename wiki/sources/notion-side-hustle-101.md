## Summary
A broad side-hustle ideation and execution notebook spanning product concepts, service businesses, creator/digital-product paths, and offline market-selling tactics. The content emphasizes rapid idea generation, low-cost testing, and selecting one idea to execute within a week.

## Key Claims
- Diversified idea capture across online, offline, and AI-enabled channels increases opportunity surface.
- Small-scale market testing (events/truck sale/booth tactics) is a practical way to validate demand quickly.
- Decision quality improves when each idea is evaluated by investment, return, and timeline before committing.

## Key Quotes
> "For each idea, list it's estimated investment and return, timeline, and then choose one to act on in the next week"

## Summary
Long long, as we stand here today, I can't help but reflect on our journey together. To be honest, I was never keen on getting married. After being a bridesmaid three times and attending countless weddings, I saw more disappointments than dreams come true. But when I am with you, one plus one felt better than being on my own. I think you are very late. I have been waiting to be at this altar since our freshman year in university, when I first asked if you wanted to marry me. Because you were an idiot back then, you did not answer. I swore to myself that I would never ask another person to marry me unless he proposed first. Ever since we reunited after ten years, I waited so long for you to propose that I almost considered proposing to myself just to speed things up! Then, finally, you did it, under the setting sun in Riverdale Park East, with about a million other people around.

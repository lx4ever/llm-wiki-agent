## Summary
### Chapter 3: The Family Secret I arrived at the grand Manor on a cloudy afternoon, its imposing structure casting long shadows over the well-manicured lawns. Hired to tutor the children of the estate, I was curious about the family, especially after my encounters with the past. The housekeeper, a stern-faced woman named Mrs. Blythe, greeted me at the door. “The children are in the study,” she said, leading me through the vast corridors lined with portraits of stern-looking ancestors.

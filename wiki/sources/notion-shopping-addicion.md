## Summary
A personal reflection page focused on reducing compulsive shopping urges through mindfulness and values-based decision-making. It frames spending control as mental freedom and long-term alignment with financial priorities.

## Key Claims
- Shopping urges can be managed by pausing and observing thoughts without judgment.
- Impulse spending often reflects conflict between instant gratification and long-term goals.
- Explicit value reminders (future savings, emergency fund, major life goals) improve decision quality.

## Key Quotes
> "What is it and how to release myself from its hold? I want the freedom of mind away from this wanting of things."
